Types of Operators
An operator is a symbol that performs a certain operation between operands.
• Arithmetic Operators :- +, -, *, /, %, **
• Relational I Comparison Operators :- ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=
• Assignment Operators :- =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=, **=
• Logical Operators :- not , and , or
